Company History/Overview

Established in 2013, Parken Solution has grown from humble beginnings to become the company it is today through an interesting and fascinating journey with evolving business solutions. We started out with a limited vision of being a local IT solution provider, and we went by the label “IT Crops”. As we expanded through our diligent services and timely solution offerings, we renamed ourselves “Parken Solution”. Parken Solution's headquarters are currently located in Southampton, England. Parken Solution uses next-generation technology to assist businesses throughout the world in transforming their operations. With a world-renowned management philosophy, a strong culture of creativity and risk-taking, and a relentless focus on customer interactions, our technological products and services are built on innovation. Parken Solution's remarkably broad and deep collection of IT capabilities can help a company accelerate its technology-led growth. We are in a unique position to better serve our clients by combining our tradition of world-class services with battle-tested and innovative products. At Parken Solution, we are eager to broaden our horizons with a new zeal, identity, and impetus.
